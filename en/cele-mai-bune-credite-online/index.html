This page is a decision framework. We are not a bank and we do not issue loans. Final terms and approval decisions belong to the lender.

If you're unsure about safety first: read the safety checklist.

Comparison criteria

  • Legitimacy & transparency: real company details, clear terms, contact information.
  • Total cost: interest, fees, penalties, total repayment example.
  • Terms: amount range, repayment period, minimum requirements.
  • Process: steps, documents, response time (varies).
  • Risk handling: what happens if you pay late and how extensions work.

Who it fits / who it doesn't

May fit if

  • You have a realistic repayment plan.
  • You understand total cost, not just monthly payment.
  • You compare multiple options before applying.

Not fit if

  • You want to cover another loan without a plan.
  • You can't handle late fees if something goes wrong.
  • You rely on "guaranteed approval" claims.
