Full guide: online loans in Romania

This is a practical explainer: how the process works, what costs mean, and what to watch out for.

If you want the quick framework instead: comparison page.

How online loans typically work

  1. You choose an option and read terms (amount, period, costs).
  2. You submit an application on the lender’s site.
  3. The lender reviews eligibility (varies).
  4. If approved, you receive final terms and complete the contract steps.

Costs: what to actually compare

  • Interest rate: what you pay for borrowing.
  • Fees: processing/admin fees (if any).
  • Penalties: late fees and how fast they add up.
  • Total repayment: the number that matters most.

If you pay late

  • Extra fees and penalties may apply.
  • Your credit record can be affected (depends on lender/reporting).
  • If you are struggling, contact the lender early and ask about options.

Safety basics

If a lender promises “guaranteed approval” or asks for upfront money, pause.
